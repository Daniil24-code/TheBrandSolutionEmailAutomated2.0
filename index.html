Section 03

Your Ideal Client

This is your client avatar — the exact person your content needs to speak to. Be as specific as you can.

* Describe your ideal client in detail
Who are they? What do they do? Where do they work? How do they spend their time? Age range, background, level of expertise?
* Where is your ideal client RIGHT NOW? (The Zero State)
What does their current situation look like? What problems are they experiencing daily? What have they tried that hasn't worked?
* Where does your ideal client WANT to be? (The Hero State)
Paint the picture of their ideal future — the outcome they're chasing. What does life look like once they achieve it?
